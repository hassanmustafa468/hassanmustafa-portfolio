About

Building hardware that ships.

I'm a Mechanical Design Engineer at AIO App Inc. — a stealth-mode startup I joined straight out of university, two years before it went public. I graduated in Mechanical Engineering from CEME, NUST (Class of 2023) and hold a CSWA certification.

Within two years of starting my career, I was on factory floors in Shenzhen, China — working days and nights to take products from T0/T1 mould samples through to mass production. Most recently, I ran a 4-week China trip covering 4 products across 3 assembly lines simultaneously: Wireless Charging Pad, Tableside AI Device, Self-Ordering KIOSK 27", and Mega KIOSK 65". My CTO's words when I landed: "Actually, now you have graduated."

I also take on freelance mechanical design projects, delivering complete packages for clients in North America. Every project is grounded in DFM and DFA — I design for how things will actually be made and assembled, not just how they look in CAD.
